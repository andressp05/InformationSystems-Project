Game Rules

  • It is a two players online game.
  • The playerâ€™s moves alternate with the cat moving first.
  • The player may move only one piece each turn.
  • The mouse is trapped when it can no longer move to a vacant square. It is possible for two cats to trap the mouse against an edge of the board (other than their original home-row) or even one corner where a single cat may do the trapping. Should a cat reach the mouse original home row it will be unable to move further.
  • 4-Cats Player:
    • Initial Position: The four cats are initially placed on the white squares at one edge of the board.
    • Objective: To prevent the mouse from arriving to any of the inital cats positions.
    • Allowed Movements: The cat move diagonally forward one square.
  • Mouse Player:
    • Initial Position: The mouse is initially placed on the second white square at the opposite edge of the board.
    • Objective: To cross from one side of the board to the other, arriving at any one of the cat original squares.
    • Allowed Movements: The mouse moves diagonally forward or backward one square.
